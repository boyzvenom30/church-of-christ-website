Our Doctrine

Our Faith & Doctrine

We believe in the complete authority of the Bible. As a non-denominational congregation, we do not adhere to human creeds or external councils. Instead, we seek to return to the simple structure, worship, and practice of the first-century church as depicted in the scriptures.

Our belief structure is centered on the following scriptural principles:

  • Bible as Sole Guide We speak where the Bible speaks, and remain silent where the Bible is silent (2 Timothy 3:16-17).
  • The Plan of Salvation Hear the word, Believe, Repent of sins, Confess Christ, and be Baptized for the remission of sins (Acts 2:38).
  • Autonomous Congregations Each local church is guided locally by a plurality of qualified elders, answering only to Christ.
Restoration Movement Goal

Restoring Apostolic Christianity

Our goal is not to start a new denomination or reform an old one, but to bypass centuries of denominational divisions and go straight back to the original seed of the gospel as found in the Bible.

By following the teachings of Jesus Christ and the apostles directly, we seek to be Christians only, united in truth and love.

History

Our Journey

The history and development of the Church of Christ congregation in Kumaran Nagar.

1

Early Beginnings

A small group of believers in Chennai began meeting in homes, dedicating themselves to prayer and the study of God's word, seeking the purity of scriptural faith.

2

Establishment in Kumaran Nagar

As the numbers grew, the church established a stable presence in Kumaran Nagar, Chennai. Regular bible classes and public worship services were commenced to serve the local neighborhood.

3

Growth & Outreach

Today, the church serves as an active community center. With robust ministries for youth, children, and benevolent outreach, we strive to support local disciples and remain faithful to the apostolic pattern.

Outreach

Mission & Community Works

Active ministries demonstrating the love of Christ in Chennai and beyond.

Free Medical Camps

We sponsor quarterly general health checkup camps in Kumaran Nagar, partnering with Christian medical professionals to provide free basic healthcare, diagnostics, and counseling to underprivileged families.

Education Sponsorship

Helping local children stay in school by providing school supplies, books, and scholarship funds to low-income families in our community, fostering positive growth and mentorship.

Disaster Relief & Aid

In times of monsoonal flooding and severe storms in Chennai, the church acts as a localized supply hub, distributing food packets, dry rations, and clean drinking water to affected families.

Ministries

Sunday School

Guiding our children in the love and wisdom of God's Word through age-appropriate scripture studies.

Primary Class: Bible Foundations

Focuses on introducing young minds to the major stories of the Bible, the creation, the love of God, and basic moral teachings. Classes use interactive crafts, simple songs, and engaging storytelling.

Quarterly Focus: Old Testament Heroes (Noah, Abraham, Moses, and David) • Simple Memory Verses.

Key Topics Covered:

  • • The Creation of the World (Genesis 1-2)
  • • The Story of Noah's Ark (Genesis 6-9)
  • • Joseph and his Multi-colored Coat
  • • Daniel in the Lions' Den
  • • Parables of Jesus told simply

Juniors: Growing in faith

Designed to help children read and locate scriptures themselves. We study the historical timeline of the Old Testament and the life and teachings of Jesus Christ in detail.

Quarterly Focus: The Life, Ministry, and Miracles of Jesus Christ in the Synoptic Gospels.

Key Topics Covered:

  • • The Books of the Bible Structure
  • • The Sermon on the Mount
  • • Jesus Calming the Storm
  • • The Parable of the Prodigal Son
  • • The Early Apostles and their calling

Seniors: Applying Scripture

Seniors are guided to apply Bible principles to their daily lives, handling school peer pressures, developing moral integrity, and understanding the scheme of redemption.

Quarterly Focus: The Book of Acts • How the Early Church spread the Gospel.

Key Topics Covered:

  • • The Day of Pentecost (Acts 2)
  • • The Journeys of the Apostle Paul
  • • Christian Armor (Ephesians 6)
  • • Peer Pressure and Decision Making
  • • Understanding Christian baptism

Youth Class: Strong Discipleship

Prepares teenagers for college and adulthood, centering on advanced biblical theology, church history, apologetics (defending the faith), and spiritual development.

Quarterly Focus: Basic Christian Apologetics • Faith vs. Skepticism in the modern world.

Key Topics Covered:

  • • The Reliability of Biblical Manuscripts
  • • Study of the Epistle to the Romans
  • • Finding God's Will for Your Career
  • • Serving and Preaching in local assemblies
  • • Biblical service qualities
